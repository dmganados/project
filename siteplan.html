Project Site Plan

Site Name

Name:One Piece: The Grand Adventure

This name represents a comprehensive fan site dedicated to the popular anime "One Piece," offering extensive information on characters, episode summaries, reviews, and the latest news.

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide a detailed and engaging resource for "One Piece" fans, featuring character profiles, episode guides, blog posts, and updates. It will serve both long-time fans and newcomers, fostering a community of enthusiasts.

Scenarios

  • What are the latest episode summaries and reviews for "One Piece"?
  • Where can I find in-depth character profiles for the Straw Hat Pirates?

Color Schema

Dark Blue: #003049 (used for headings and accents)
Red: #D62828 (used for headings and accents)
Orange: #F77F00 (used for highlights and accents)
Light Yellow: #EAE2B7 (used for background)

Typography

Heading Font: 'DM Sans', sans-serif (used for headings)

Body Font: 'DM Sans', sans-serif (used for body text)
